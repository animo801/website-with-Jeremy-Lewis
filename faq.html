What is a water softener?

A water softener is a simple device converts hard water into soft water. Hard water, or water that comes straight from your pipes, causes long term damage to a home. Soft water eliminates this damage as well as providing water that is gentle on the skin for showers, baths, and cooking.

How do I know if I have a water softener?

Most homes built after 2001 have water softeners. If you are new to living in your home, the best thing to do would have us come out for a free inspection.

Why should I fill my water softener?

If you have a water softener and don't keep it filled, two things are happening. First, your pipes are being damaged due to hard water. Second, your water softener is wasting water. Filling it causes both of these issues.
